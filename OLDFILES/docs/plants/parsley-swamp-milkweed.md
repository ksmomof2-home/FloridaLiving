  <div class="plant-facts">
    <h3>Quick Facts</h3>
    <ul>
      <li><strong>USDA zones:</strong> Parsley: all • Milkweed: 3–9</li>
      <li><strong>Sun:</strong> Full sun to part shade</li>
      <li><strong>Water:</strong> Average (milkweed likes it moist)</li>
      <li><strong>Height:</strong> Parsley 1–2 ft • Milkweed 3–5 ft</li>
      <li><strong>Spacing:</strong> 2–3 plants per 30-gal pot</li>
      <li><strong>Let flower!</strong> → caterpillar city</li>
    </ul>
    <p><strong>Primary Use:</strong> Double butterfly host → Black Swallowtail + Monarch</p>
  </div>
</div>

<div class="full-width">
  <h3>Wildlife Supported</h3>
  <ul>
    <li>Parsley → Black Swallowtail host</li>
    <li>Swamp Milkweed → Monarch host (only food for caterpillars!)</li>
    <li>Nectar for bees & butterflies</li>
  </ul>

  <h3>Pests to Watch</h3>
  <ul>
    <li>Swallowtail & Monarch caterpillars → **DO NOT KILL** — they’re the whole point!</li>
  </ul>

  <h3>Unusual / Fun Facts</h3>
  <ul>
    <li>Swamp milkweed smells like vanilla when blooming</li>
    <li>Parsley is a biennial — let it flower in year 2 for extra swallowtail action</li>
  </ul>

  <h3>Local Care Tips (The Villages 9b)</h3>
  <ul>
    <li>Pos 7 — future home of the butterfly buffet</li>
    <li>Parsley currently in small pot 5a → moving soon</li>
    <li>Swamp milkweed coming soon</li>
  </ul>
</div>
