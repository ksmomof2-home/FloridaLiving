# Basil – Genovese & Thai
**Ocimum basilicum** | Lamiaceae

![Basil plants]({{ "/assets/basil.jpg" | relative_url }})

### Quick Facts
- Sun: Full sun
- Height: 18–30 inches
- Harvest: Pinch tips regularly

### Primary Use
- Culinary → pesto, Thai curries, caprese

### Local Care Tips
- Pos 11 final destination
- Current basil plant in small pot 7a → moving soon
- Thai basil loves our heat
