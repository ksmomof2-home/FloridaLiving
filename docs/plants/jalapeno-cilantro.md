# Jalapeño + Cilantro
**Capsicum annuum** + **Coriandrum sativum** | Solanaceae + Apiaceae

![Jalapeño with cilantro]({{ "/assets/jalapeno_pepper_cilantro.jpg" | relative_url }})

### Quick Facts
- Sun: Full sun
- Jalapeño height: 2–3 ft · Cilantro: 1–2 ft
- Days to harvest: Jalapeño 70–80 · Cilantro 50–60

### Primary Use
- Culinary → fresh salsa, poppers, tacos

### Local Care Tips
- Pos 9 – cilantro + marigold seeds planted Nov 22
- Jalapeño transplant coming soon
- Cilantro loves the shade under the pepper
