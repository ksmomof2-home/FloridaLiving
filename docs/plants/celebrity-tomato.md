# Celebrity Tomato
**Solanum lycopersicum 'Celebrity'** | Solanaceae

<img src = "https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ksmomof2-home/FloridaLiving/main/docs/assets/pot2.jpg" width = 400 alt = "Celebrity tomato">

### Quick Facts
- Type: Hybrid determinate → grows 3–4 ft in pot
- Days to harvest: 70–75 from transplant
- Fruit size: 8–12 oz globes
- Sun: Full sun (Pos 2 – perfect!)
- Water: Consistent, never let dry out completely

### Primary Use
- Culinary → slicing, salads, sandwiches

### Pests to Watch (The Villages)
- Whiteflies → foil + soap spray + sticky traps
- Hornworms → hand-pick or BT

### Local Care Tips
- 2 plants per 30-gal pot (exactly what we’re doing)
- Mulch with pine bark + aluminum foil underneath
- Side-dress with 6-6-6 every 4 weeks
- Arriving ~Dec 1, 2025
